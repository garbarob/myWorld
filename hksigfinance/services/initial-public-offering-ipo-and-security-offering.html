Initial Public Offering (IPO) and Security Offering

Going public is a complicated, costly, time-consuming and stressful process. However, Hong Kong Swiss Investment Group experts managed every step required to complete an Initial Public Offering. When resorting to us to administer the process, our clients corporate objectives can be achieved faster, easier and often at a lower cost.

Our capital markets consultants advise issuers, underwriters, placement agents and initial purchasers in the public and private offerings of securities. We offer our financial markets clients in-depth understanding and expertise in every major industry.

Hong Kong Swiss Investment Group professionals develop a proactive, systematic and integrated approach to applying corporate governance and compliance requirements. We encourage companies to implement corporate governance standards not only as regulatory obligations but as value drivers as well.
